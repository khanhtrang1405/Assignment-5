About Us

A Solar Citizen is any Australian who supports the use of solar and renewable power â€“ either installing it themselves or working to see greater use of solar in their community.We are a community of solar lovers, we are Solar Citizens!

Solar Citizens is an independent, community-based organisation working to protect and grow renewable energy in Australia.

Our activities include raising awareness about renewable energy through events, reports, materials and campaigns. People can get involved through donations or volunteering to help with the events or to share with families and friends about the NGO or benefits of solar energy.



As of today, Australia has more than 2 million solar photovoltaic installations across Australia. From our 2019-2020 annual report, we has grown our supporter base to more than 10,700 people. Also, 118,111 people have joined the community. In 2019, we received $652,805 (97,90% of total income) from donations, appeals and merchandise sales.



Our missions


  • Creating a community to connect all solar lovers

  • Protect solar users for their rights

  • Every Australians to be able to access to solar energy
