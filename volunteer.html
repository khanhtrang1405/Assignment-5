Volunteer

One of the most important things you can do as a volunteer is help to build the Solar Citizens movement. Our supporters and volunteers are at the heart of our organisation. There is a role for everyone, and we'd love to hear from you.


  • Why?

    Reason 1

    As a volunteer, you take part in building the Solar Citizens movement - this national network of solar owners, solar supporters and local solar action groups.


    Reason 2

    Together, we take action to protect the rights of Australia’s 2 and a half million solar homes and businesses, and pursue the largest opportunities for a fast and fair transition to clean, renewable energy.

  • How?

    Sign Up

    Sign up with the form for news about our one off events and campaigns near your local areas.



    Share

    Are you a regular user of facebook, twitter or instagram? Or perhaps you’d be happy to share your story to promote the benefits of solar energy? We’d love digital volunteers all across Australia.
